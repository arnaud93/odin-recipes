Lebanese Hummus

Description

Learning to make the perfect hummus is a matter of trial and error.
There are little shops all over Lebanon and the Levant that specialise in just hummus and foul (fava beans),
which I especially love visiting for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas
  • 1 tbsp bicarbonate of soda + 1 tsp
  • 1/2 cup of tahini
  • 2 large lemons
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1-2 gloves of garlic
  • 2 ice cubes

How to make Hummus

  1. Soak chickpeas
  2. Rinse chickpeas
  3. Bring to a boil
  4. Drain chickpeas
  5. Remove skins
  6. Put chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, salt, olive oil in the food processor
  7. Taste hummus etc
